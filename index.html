My name is Maximilian (Max) Wolff and I am a sophomore at Wesleyan University. I am working in Wieland Brendel's lab at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems. I'm interested in gaining a fundamental, formal understanding of how machine learning models function.

I've been fortunate enough to have three incredible research mentors, Professor Christopher Fletcher, Mahmood Sharif, and my very own father, Stuart Wolff.
